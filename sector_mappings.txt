SECTOR RISK MAPS (12 SECTORS): RISK FACTORS • LEADING INDICATORS • CONTAGION PATHS

Use this as a fast translation layer from “news about X” to “which risks move, what to watch next, and where contagion likely travels.” Each sector includes: (1) common risk factors, (2) leading indicators (quant/qual), and (3) typical contagion paths (who gets hit next).

===============================================================================
1) TECHNOLOGY
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Valuation/duration risk (rates sensitivity), platform regulation/antitrust, cybersecurity, supply-chain constraints (semis), AI model/IP risks, customer IT budget cycles.
Leading indicators
- 10Y yields / real rates, NASDAQ breadth, capex guidance from hyperscalers, semiconductor book-to-bill, enterprise renewal rates, breach disclosures, regulatory actions.
Contagion paths
- Tech selloff → VC funding slowdown → startups/SMEs cut spend → commercial real estate (office) and payroll services; semis weakness → industrial automation demand slows.

===============================================================================
2) FINANCIALS (BANKS, INSURERS, BROKER-DEALERS)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Credit losses (consumer/CRE/corporate), funding/liquidity (deposits, wholesale), interest rate risk (NIM + duration), regulatory capital changes, counterparty risk, operational/fraud.
Leading indicators
- Deposit flows, repo/CP rates, CDS spreads, yield curve slope, delinquency and charge-off rates, CRE vacancy and cap rates, capital ratios, rating actions.
Contagion paths
- Bank stress → tighter lending standards → SMEs/cyclicals weaken → unemployment rises → consumer credit worsens; market volatility → broker leverage reduction → asset price liquidation.

===============================================================================
3) HEALTHCARE (PROVIDERS, PHARMA, BIOTECH)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Policy/reimbursement changes, patent cliffs, clinical/regulatory trial risk, litigation, labor costs (providers), supply chain of critical inputs.
Leading indicators
- FDA/EMA calendars, legislative headlines, payer utilization metrics, wage inflation for nurses, drug pricing scrutiny, pipeline trial readouts.
Contagion paths
- Reimbursement cuts → provider margins down → muni finances (hospital systems) → regional bank credit; pharma patent loss → M&A waves and biotech funding shifts.

===============================================================================
4) ENERGY (OIL, GAS, REFINING, SERVICES)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Commodity price shocks, geopolitical supply disruptions, OPEC policy, regulatory/climate transition, cost inflation, reserve replacement and capex cycles.
Leading indicators
- Brent/WTI levels and term structure, inventory data, rig counts, crack spreads, shipping disruptions, sanctions headlines, carbon pricing policy.
Contagion paths
- Oil spike → inflation → rate hikes → broad risk-off; oil crash → high-yield energy defaults → credit spreads widen → funding costs rise for cyclicals.

===============================================================================
5) INDUSTRIALS
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Global growth/trade cycles, supply-chain fragility, input costs, order cancellations, defense spending shifts, labor availability.
Leading indicators
- PMIs, new orders/backlogs, freight rates, industrial metals, capex plans, export orders, government procurement.
Contagion paths
- Industrial downturn → layoffs → consumer spending weakens; supplier distress → production halts in autos/electronics; trade restrictions → retaliation impacts agriculture/tech.

===============================================================================
6) CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Household income/employment, credit availability, inflation, shifting preferences, inventory mismanagement, FX (for global brands).
Leading indicators
- Retail sales, consumer confidence, credit card delinquencies, wage growth, promotional intensity, shipping volumes, housing turnover.
Contagion paths
- Credit tightening → discretionary demand falls → retail bankruptcies → commercial landlords and banks take losses; fuel/food inflation → spending shifts to staples.

===============================================================================
7) CONSUMER STAPLES
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Input cost inflation (commodities, packaging), pricing power/elasticity, retailer bargaining power, FX translation, supply disruptions.
Leading indicators
- Commodity baskets, price/mix commentary, discounting trends, inventory levels at retailers, emerging-market FX, shipping and logistics costs.
Contagion paths
- Staples inflation → political pressure (price controls) → margins compress; supply shocks → shortages → consumer sentiment declines → discretionary also suffers.

===============================================================================
8) UTILITIES
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Interest rate sensitivity (capital intensive), regulatory rate cases, fuel input costs, grid reliability, climate events, stranded asset risk (transition).
Leading indicators
- Credit spreads, regulator filings, outage metrics, weather patterns, natural gas prices, capex and allowed ROE decisions.
Contagion paths
- Rate spikes → utility valuations fall → refinancing stress for leveraged utilities; extreme weather → insured losses + infrastructure repair demand → materials/industrials.

===============================================================================
9) REAL ESTATE (REITS, PROPERTY DEVELOPERS)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Rates and cap rates, refinancing walls, occupancy (office/retail), construction costs, tenant credit, local regulation/taxes.
Leading indicators
- Cap rate moves, refinancing volumes, CMBS spreads, vacancy/lease renewals, rent growth, construction starts, bank lending standards.
Contagion paths
- CRE stress → regional banks (loan losses) → credit tightening; falling property values → local fiscal strain → muni risk; construction slowdown → materials and labor demand drops.

===============================================================================
10) MATERIALS (CHEMICALS, METALS, MINING)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Commodity cycles, China/global demand, energy costs, environmental regulation, project execution, geopolitical supply concentration.
Leading indicators
- Industrial metals prices, Chinese credit impulse/PMIs, inventory-to-sales, freight indices, permitting headlines, power prices.
Contagion paths
- Materials downturn → mining capex cuts → industrial equipment demand falls; supply disruption (rare earths) → tech/defense production constraints.

===============================================================================
11) TELECOMMUNICATION SERVICES (TELCOS, COMMUNICATIONS)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Leverage and refinancing, spectrum/regulatory decisions, capex intensity (5G/fiber), competition/price wars, network outages and cybersecurity.
Leading indicators
- ARPU trends, churn, spectrum auction news, capex guidance, credit spreads, outage reports, regulator actions.
Contagion paths
- Price war → margins down → vendor spending cut (equipment makers); outage/cyber event → regulatory penalties → broader critical infrastructure scrutiny.

===============================================================================
12) TRANSPORTATION & LOGISTICS (AIRLINES, SHIPPING, TRUCKING)
===============================================================================
Risk factors
- Fuel costs, demand cyclicality, labor disputes, capacity management, geopolitical chokepoints, debt load, safety/regulatory incidents.
Leading indicators
- Jet fuel spreads, booking data, load factors, container rates, port congestion, PMI/export orders, strike headlines, accident investigations.
Contagion paths
- Chokepoint disruption → inventory shortages → retail/industrial production hits; freight collapse → trucker defaults → bank/lessor losses; airline distress → aerospace supplier weakness.

===============================================================================
Cross-sector shortcuts for contagion recognition
===============================================================================
- Funding shock (banks/markets): Financials → Real Estate → Industrials/Discretionary → Labor market → Consumer credit.
- Commodity shock (energy/materials): Energy/Materials → Inflation → Rates → Tech/Utilities/Real Estate valuations.
- Policy/regulatory shock: Healthcare/Financials/Tech → broad multiples reset → M&A/funding changes across growth sectors.
- Cyber/operational shock: Tech/Telecom → critical infrastructure → regulatory tightening → capex reprioritization in Industrials/Utilities.
